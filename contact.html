Hi, I'm Ayesha — an artist based in India. My work is shaped by both inner creativity and the beauty that exists in the world around us. I explore different forms of art including calligraphy, sketches, paintings, and handmade crafts.

I enjoy working across styles, languages, and mediums — from acrylic and watercolor paintings to detailed, intricate artworks. As a creator, I focus on keeping my work authentic while still making it visually meaningful and expressive.

I put a lot of emotion into everything I create. I genuinely love my art — sometimes so much that I catch myself smiling at it, blowing kisses, and quietly saying "I love you" to a finished piece.

I'm drawn to art that feels personal and intentional. Whether it's a simple piece or something more detailed, I value the process just as much as the final result.

I'm open to custom projects and creative opportunities. Feel free to reach out with ideas, questions, or just to connect.
